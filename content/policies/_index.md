# Program Policies & Standards

All Core Values Recovery Level 1 ACC participants must review and acknowledge these policies as part of the enrollment process.

## Required Policies

### Academic Policies
- [Enrollment Agreement](enrollment-agreement/) - Terms and conditions for program participation
- [Partial Completion Policy](partial-completion-policy/) - Guidelines for participants who don't complete the full program
- [Transfer of Credit Policy](transfer-of-credit-policy/) - Transferring hours from other ICF programs
- [Illness Policy](illness-policy/) - Accommodations for medical circumstances

### Financial Policies
- [Payment & Fees Policy](payment-fees-policy/) - Tuition payment plans and late fees
- [Refund Policy](refund-policy/) - Refund eligibility and timelines

### Standards & Compliance
- [Disability, Discrimination & DEIJ Statement](disability-deij-statement/) - Our commitment to inclusive education
- [Complaint & Grievance Policy](complaint-grievance-policy/) - Process for addressing concerns
- [Ethical Marketing Agreement](ethical-marketing-agreement/) - Standards for representing CVR credentials
- [Ethical Standards](ethical-standards/) - Organizational integrity and transparency standards
- [Data Privacy & GDPR Consent](gdpr-consent/) - Student data handling and consent

## ICF Compliance

All policies align with International Coaching Federation (ICF) Level 1 Accredited Coach Training Program standards.
