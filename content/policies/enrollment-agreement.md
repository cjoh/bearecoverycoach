# Core Values Recovery Level 1 ACC Program
## Enrollment Agreement

**Effective Date:** [To be determined]

### Purpose
This Enrollment Agreement establishes the mutual commitments between Core Values Recovery (CVR) and participants in the Level 1 ACC Coach Training Program.

### Program Details
- **Program:** Core Values Recovery Level 1 ACC Coach Training
- **Duration:** 20 weeks
- **Total Hours:** 80 hours coach-specific training
- **Format:** Hybrid (synchronous and asynchronous learning)
- **ICF Alignment:** Meets ICF Level 1 Accreditation standards

### Participant Commitments
By enrolling, participants agree to:

1. **Attendance Requirements**
   - Attend all scheduled synchronous training sessions
   - Complete asynchronous learning modules within specified timeframes
   - Participate actively in practice coaching sessions and group activities

2. **Practice Requirements**
   - Complete all required practice coaching sessions
   - Submit required recordings for mentor coaching observation
   - Engage in peer coaching as assigned

3. **Academic Integrity**
   - Complete all assignments independently unless group work is specified
   - Maintain confidentiality of all practice client information
   - Adhere to ICF Code of Ethics throughout the program

4. **Professional Conduct**
   - Treat all participants, instructors, and staff with respect
   - Maintain appropriate professional boundaries
   - Communicate openly and honestly about challenges or concerns

### CVR Commitments
Core Values Recovery agrees to:

1. **Quality Education**
   - Provide ICF-aligned curriculum delivered by qualified instructors
   - Offer 80 hours of coach-specific training meeting Level 1 standards
   - Provide access to learning materials and resources

2. **Support Services**
   - Assign mentor coaches for required observation and feedback
   - Provide timely feedback on assignments and performance
   - Offer reasonable accommodations for documented needs

3. **Performance Evaluation**
   - Assess participant competency against ICF ACC standards
   - Provide clear feedback on areas of strength and growth
   - Issue certificates of completion upon successful program completion

### Completion Requirements
To successfully complete the program, participants must:

- Attend minimum 90% of synchronous sessions
- Complete all required asynchronous modules
- Demonstrate competency in all ICF Core Competencies at ACC level
- Complete required practice coaching hours
- Pass final performance evaluation
- Maintain compliance with all program policies

### Certificate of Completion
Upon successful completion, participants receive:
- Official CVR Level 1 ACC Program certificate
- Documentation of 80 hours coach-specific training
- Portfolio of evidence for ICF ACC credential application

### Modification of Agreement
CVR reserves the right to modify program requirements with 30 days notice to enrolled participants. Material changes will be communicated in writing.
