# Core Values Recovery Level 1 ACC Program
## Illness and Absence Policy

**Effective Date:** [To be determined]

### Purpose
This policy provides guidelines for managing illness, personal emergencies, and absences during the CVR Level 1 ACC Coach Training Program while maintaining program integrity and ICF accreditation standards.

### Attendance Requirements

#### Minimum Attendance
To successfully complete the program, participants must:
- Attend at least 90% of synchronous training sessions
- Complete 100% of asynchronous learning modules
- Participate in all required practice coaching and observation sessions

**For an 80-hour program:**
- Maximum allowable absence: 8 hours of synchronous training
- All asynchronous work must be completed (flexible scheduling)

### Types of Absences

#### Planned Absences
Scheduled absences known in advance (medical appointments, work obligations, family events):

**Notification Required:**
- Submit written notice to Program Director at least 2 weeks in advance
- Include:
  - Date(s) of absence
  - Reason (brief, documentation not required for short absences)
  - Plan for making up missed work

**Make-Up Options:**
- Review session recording (if available)
- Complete alternative assignment covering session content
- Attend same session in future cohort (if available)
- One-on-one instruction session (may incur additional fee)

#### Unplanned Absences
Unexpected absences due to illness or emergency:

**Notification Required:**
- Contact Program Director as soon as possible
- Email: [Contact email]
- Phone/Text: [Contact phone]
- Within 24 hours of missed session preferred

**Documentation:**
- Not required for single absence
- May be required for extended absence (3+ sessions)
- Medical note or other verification at Program Director's discretion

### Illness-Related Absences

#### Short-Term Illness (1-2 sessions)
- Notify Program Director before session start time
- No documentation required
- Review recordings and complete make-up work
- Communicate with mentor coach if practice sessions affected

#### Extended Illness (3-7 days)
- Provide medical note or documentation
- Program Director creates individualized catch-up plan
- May include:
  - Extended deadlines for assignments
  - Rescheduled practice sessions
  - Modified participation schedule
- Timeline for completion discussed and agreed upon

#### Serious Illness/Hospitalization (1+ weeks)
- Medical documentation required
- Options include:
  - **Program deferral:** Move to next cohort with no penalty
  - **Extended timeline:** Complete program over longer period
  - **Partial completion:** Document hours completed, re-enroll when able
  - **Medical withdrawal:** Full withdrawal with refund consideration (see Refund Policy)

**Decision factors:**
- Severity and expected recovery timeline
- Participant's preference and readiness
- Impact on program integrity and cohort
- Medical provider recommendations

### Family and Personal Emergencies

#### Immediate Family Illness/Emergency
Absences due to serious illness or emergency involving immediate family (spouse, children, parents, siblings):
- Notify Program Director as soon as possible
- Flexible make-up arrangements provided
- Documentation may be requested for extended absence
- Same options as extended illness above

#### Bereavement
In the event of death of immediate family member:
- Up to 2 weeks excused absence
- No documentation required
- Program timeline extended as needed
- Re-engagement plan created when participant is ready
- Full support and compassion provided

### Mental Health and Wellness

#### Mental Health Days
CVR recognizes importance of mental health:
- Participants may take mental health days as needed
- Treated the same as physical illness
- Counted toward maximum absence allowance
- No judgment, full support provided

#### Recovery-Related Challenges
For participants in recovery facing challenges:
- **Crisis situation:** Immediate support, program deferral available
- **Relapse:** Compassionate response, focus on stabilization first
- **Treatment admission:** Automatic program deferral, re-engage when stable
- Confidentiality maintained

**Note:** CVR's mission is supporting recovery. We will NEVER penalize participants for prioritizing their recovery and wellness.

### Making Up Missed Work

#### Synchronous Sessions
Options for making up missed sessions:
1. **Session Recordings** (when available)
   - Access within 48 hours of session
   - Complete reflection assignment on session content
   - Submit within 1 week of original session date

2. **Future Cohort Attendance**
   - Attend same session in next program cohort
   - Free of charge for up to 2 sessions
   - Subject to availability

3. **Individual Instruction**
   - One-on-one with instructor or mentor
   - Covers missed material
   - Fee: $[amount] per hour
   - Scheduled within 2 weeks of absence

#### Asynchronous Modules
- No make-up required (self-paced)
- Extended deadline may be granted
- Must complete before final evaluation

#### Practice Coaching Sessions
- Reschedule with practice partner
- Coordinate with mentor coach for observations
- Must complete all required sessions before program end

### Extended Absence Management

#### Absence Exceeding Maximum Allowance
If participant misses more than 10% of synchronous training:

**Options:**
1. **Complete make-up work** to satisfy all missed hours
2. **Defer to next cohort** and attend missed sessions
3. **Partial completion** with reduced hours documented
4. **Withdrawal** with refund eligibility per Refund Policy

**Decision process:**
- Meet with Program Director
- Assess remaining requirements
- Create realistic completion plan
- Sign amended enrollment agreement if timeline extended

### Chronic or Recurring Absences

#### Pattern of Absences
If participant develops pattern of frequent absences:
- Program Director initiates conversation
- Assess whether participant can realistically complete program
- Explore barriers and solutions
- May recommend:
  - Program deferral to better timing
  - Reduced course load (if future options available)
  - Withdrawal with re-enrollment later

**Goal:** Support participant success, not punish challenges.

### Impact on Program Completion

#### Completion Timeline
Standard program: 20 weeks

**With absences, completion may extend to:**
- Up to 26 weeks with make-up work
- Up to 12 months with illness deferral
- Beyond 12 months requires special approval

#### Certificate Issuance
Certificate issued when:
- All attendance requirements met (90% synchronous)
- All make-up work completed satisfactorily
- All assignments and evaluations submitted
- Performance meets ACC standards

**Timeline delays:** Certificate issued upon completion, regardless of extended timeline.

### COVID-19 and Infectious Illness

#### Infectious Illness Protocol
Participants experiencing symptoms of COVID-19 or other contagious illness:
- **Do not attend in-person sessions**
- Participate virtually if feeling well enough
- If unable to participate, treated as illness absence
- No penalty for prioritizing health and safety

### Communication Expectations

#### Participant Responsibility
- Notify CVR as soon as possible when absence occurs
- Respond to Program Director outreach within 3 business days
- Maintain communication about recovery timeline
- Proactively request support or accommodations

#### CVR Responsibility
- Respond to absence notifications within 24 hours
- Provide clear make-up plan and options
- Follow up on participant wellness (respectfully)
- Maintain flexibility and compassion

### Confidentiality and Privacy

All medical and personal information shared regarding absences is:
- Kept strictly confidential
- Shared only with those who need to know (instructor, mentor coach)
- Not included in academic records unless participant requests
- Protected under applicable privacy laws
