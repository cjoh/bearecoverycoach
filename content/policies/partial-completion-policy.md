# Core Values Recovery Level 1 ACC Program
## Partial Completion Policy

**Effective Date:** [To be determined]

### Purpose
This policy outlines the process and requirements for participants who complete portions of the CVR Level 1 ACC Program but do not fulfill all completion requirements.

### Scope
This policy applies to participants who:
- Withdraw before program completion
- Are unable to complete all requirements within the program timeframe
- Do not meet performance standards for full completion
- Request documentation for partial participation

### Partial Completion Certificate

#### Eligibility
Participants who complete at least 40 hours (50%) of the program may request a Partial Completion Certificate documenting the specific training received.

#### Certificate Contents
Partial completion documentation includes:
- Exact number of hours completed (broken down by synchronous/asynchronous)
- Specific modules or competencies covered
- Dates of participation
- Clear statement that full program requirements were not met
- Note that partial completion does not qualify for ICF Level 1 accreditation credit

### Documentation Requirements

#### For Partial Completion Certificate
Participants must:
1. Submit written request to Program Director
2. Be current on all financial obligations
3. Return all program materials marked as CVR property
4. Complete exit interview (optional but encouraged)

#### Processing Timeline
- Requests processed within 15 business days of receipt
- Certificate issued via email and mail
- Digital records maintained for 7 years

### Re-enrollment Options

#### Continuing the Program
Participants with partial completion may re-enroll under the following conditions:

**Within 12 Months:**
- Credit given for all completed hours and modules
- Pay only for remaining program components
- Must complete within 18 months of original enrollment date

**After 12 Months:**
- Case-by-case review required
- Credit may be given for completed modules if curriculum unchanged
- Subject to current program pricing
- May be required to repeat certain modules if significant updates have occurred

#### Re-enrollment Process
1. Contact Program Director to discuss status
2. Submit re-enrollment application
3. Review updated curriculum and requirements
4. Sign updated enrollment agreement
5. Complete payment arrangement for remaining components

### Hours Credit for Other Programs

#### ICF Accredited Programs
Partial completion hours from CVR:
- May be accepted by other ICF accredited programs at their discretion
- Participant responsible for providing documentation
- CVR cannot guarantee acceptance by other programs
- Participants should verify transferability before enrolling elsewhere

#### CVR Advanced Programs
Partial completion of Level 1:
- Hours may count toward future CVR program requirements
- Subject to Program Director approval
- Must be relevant to advanced program content
- Maximum 50% of advanced program hours may come from partial completion credit

### Academic Record Notation

#### Transcript Information
Partial completion is recorded as:
- "Partial Completion - [X] hours completed of 80-hour program"
- Modules completed listed individually
- No overall grade or competency rating assigned
- Reason for non-completion (if participant chooses to share)

### Participant Rights

Participants with partial completion have the right to:
- Accurate documentation of hours and modules completed
- Fair consideration for re-enrollment
- Privacy regarding reasons for non-completion
- Access to academic records for 7 years

### Appeals Process

If a participant disputes partial completion status or documentation:
1. Submit written appeal to Program Director within 30 days
2. Include specific concerns and supporting documentation
3. Receive written response within 15 business days
4. May escalate to [CVR Leadership/Board] if not resolved
