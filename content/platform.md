## RecoveryCapital Assessment: Evidence-Based Progress Tracking

### What It Is:

Recovery capital = Internal and external resources that support recovery.

**Internal Resources:**
- Physical health
- Mental/emotional well-being
- Self-esteem, identity, sense of purpose
- Coping skills
- Spirituality/values alignment

**External Resources:**
- Social support (family, friends, recovery community)
- Housing, employment, financial stability
- Access to healthcare, therapy, recovery services
- Safe environment
- Meaningful activities

### The Assessment:

Validated questionnaire (research-backed) that measures recovery capital across multiple domains.

**Takes:** 10-15 minutes  
**Scoring:** Automatic (no manual calculation)  
**Output:** Visual dashboard showing strengths + gaps

### How You Use It:

**Intake (First Session):**
- Baseline assessment
- Identifies priorities
- Where to focus coaching energy

**Progress Tracking (Monthly or Quarterly):**
- Re-assess same domains
- See growth over time
- Celebrate wins (housing secured, new job, rebuilt relationship)
- Spot declining areas (isolation increasing, stress rising)

**Coaching Conversations:**
- Data-driven, not gut-feel
- "Your recovery capital score in social support went from 4 to 7â€”let's talk about what changed"
- Visual proof of progress (motivating for clients who feel stuck)

### Why It Matters:

**Traditional approach:** "How are you doing?" (vague, subjective)  
**RecoveryCapital approach:** "Let's look at your data" (specific, objective)

Clients can SEE their progress. Numbers don't lie. When someone says "I'm not getting better," you can show them: housing improved, employment improved, relationships improved. Recovery IS happening.
